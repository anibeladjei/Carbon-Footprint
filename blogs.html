AnC Mall, Accra. Solar Panel installation for Production

How can solar panels reduce carbon footprint?

Solar panels are capable of generating power with zero emissions. The generated power can be used to feed your energy demand, perfectly replacing conventional energy needs with green energy. Every kilowatt of green energy can reduce your carbon footprint by 3,000 pounds annually.

What Is The Carbon Footprint Of Solar Panels?

Solar panels produce lifecycle carbon dioxide emissions of about 40g equivalent for every kilowatt hour of energy they generate.

Environmental Achievements

1. Reduced greenhouse gas emissions: 2-5 tons CO2/year

1. Air pollution reduction: 1-3 tons particulate matter/year

Energy Savings:

1. Electricity savings: 50-100% of energy consumption

2. Energy independence: Reduced reliance on grid electricity

Economic Benefits:

Low maintenance costs: $100-$300/year
